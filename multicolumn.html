Multi-Column Layout

Click here to see it in action!

Description:
Two-column layout with one three-column schedule page. Contains a fixed header, a side navigation that navigates to anchor points throughout the page, changing hover icons for style, and an interactive map element.

Challenges:
Ensuring that the header and nav stayed fixed at the top without creating any horizontal scrolling. Anchoring to the correct points on the page to forgive for the extra room at the top of the body. Creating a three-column layout on one of the pages.

Things I'm Proud Of:
Creating a fixed header that is consistent throughout each page and looks great on the three-column layout as well. Embedded a Google Maps element on the Contact Us page to accompany the address. Custom bullet points that look like a photo is being taken when you hover over them.

What It's Taught Me:
Fixed headers in a fixed layout. How to embed various elements from other sources into your website. How to vary the layout from page to page while staying consistent with the brand and functionality.
