    Meal Options

    There are various meal options that we offer from vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, pescatarian, poultry only, etc. We offer a wide array of meal options as well as a choice between 2 meals during each meal. Below is an example of a typical meal plan for no specifications.

  • Breakfast: Pancakes OR Eggs and Bacon
  • Lunch: Turkey Club OR Cobb Salad
  • Dinner: Big Meatballs OR Tacos
    •

    You will be able to make your choice before the day begins so that you don't have to worry about your food all day and can instead just focus on your pictures and camera!


    Session Examples

    Woohoo! This is when you actually get to take a lot of cool pictures and learn about a lot of fun things about your camera! It's the moment you've been waiting for! Yay! Can't wait to take pix and take cool lessons from cool people!!!!! Yay! Below are some of the courses that we offer.

  • Get to Know Your Camera:
    A course about the inner and outer of your camera and how to use it and beginner tips and tricks and in depth stuff about your camera.
  • Portrait Lessons:
    Learn how to take portraits of all of your favorite people and your not favorite people! It's just! People pictures, but really good ones!
  • Editing Seminar:
    We will learn how to use software like CameraEditorXL and YayI'mEditing! They are fun to use and you will have fun learning!
  • A Picture is Worth A Million Words:
    Showing you how to tell a story with your pictures! You'll learn all about composition and the rule of thirds like you have a million times in the past! Yay! Thirds!
    •

    A full list of courses will be provided for you to choose from at the beginning of the day. This way you will be able to look at all of the courses and decide which courses are best for you and will be most beneficial!!!!


    Optional Tours

    Throughout different times, we will offer optional photo tours. These are often at oportune times like star light time and fish time and also that cool time when someone gets stuck in a well! We will take tours with groups of people that want to go and we will take pictures in fun, optimal moments! Can't wait to have you around!
