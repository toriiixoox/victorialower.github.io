Who We Are

We are the Humane Society. We have been in business for over 30 years. We are committed to finding great homes for great animals and always have been. It is our honor to be able to talk to all of you and see the beautiful community and be able to connect with all of you. We were founded in the 1980s and have been going strong ever since. We are run by amazing people and have such a great staff. Come visit us!

Adopt, Don't Shop!

In the following article, we will explain the reasons that it is always better to adopt than to shop!

1. Strong First Reason

First, it is really important that you understand the first reason. This is one of the reasons that it is super important to adopt and to not shop. If you adopt, it is also very important to know this.

2. Another Reason

You may have thought this reason was common sense, but sometimes it is not. It's hard to think about reasons such as this one when you're making big decisions and are already stressed with big choices such as responsibilities and breeds.

3. Yet Another Good Reason

Did you know that this reason is the most unknown reason of them all? Studies show that reason 3 of this list is the one that people don't know the most, but the one that people say that they wish that they had know the most. Crazy, right?

4. Would You Look at That? Another Reason

This reason is very good to know because it reminds us of all of the other reasons, but not in a bad way, in a reaffirming way, but not in a way that allows us to have a wrap up, because that's the next reason.

5. Last Reason

Finally, you have to do this because of this reason. It is the driving point and I hope that it really makes ya think about all of the other reasons as well as this one! Thanks for reading!
