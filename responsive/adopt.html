WE LOVE ANIMALS!

Animals are the light of our lives. They are so important to us. They are an extension of our family and I hope they will bring the same joy for you as well. Animals are so sweet, kind, and loving. There is no love quite like the unconditional love from a pet. They inspire us everyday. They deserve every bit of love that they get and then some. Adopt an animal today and bring them the same love!

Featured Animals for Adoption

These animals are featured for this week! They are all very unique and loving, but some are brand new and others have been hanging out at the shelter longer than others! Either way, they all want to come home with you!

Linus

Linus is 15 years old and just lookin' for someone to snuggle with and watch Wheel of Fortune. He will also settle for Jeopardy, the Voice, Deal or No Deal, or the Bachelor.

Capicola

Capicola is 1 year old and got his name from the copious amounts of deli meats he loves to consume. His favorite, though he doesn't like to chose favorites, is lightly peppered salami.

Lucy

Lucy is 5 years old and is the sweetest baby you'll ever meet in the entire world. He loves to purr and nap and knock water glasses off of the counter tops for fun.

Slate

Slate is 10 years old and just wants someone to play ball with her. Any ball that you would like to play with her, she's game. She loves golf, baseball, tenis, bowling, and more.

Christian Bale

Christian Bale was put up for adoption 2 years ago and has yet to find his forever home. He is 46 years old and has won an Academy Award and two Golden Globes. He enjoys tea and crumpets.

Yogurt

Yogurt is a 6 month year old puppy. We found her in a box by the side of the road. Since then, she's gained a good amount of weight and has perked right up. She loves to play and would love to come home with you!
